Mullak — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mullak (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the Mullak mobile application (the “App”), a marketplace that connects property buyers and renters directly with owners. By using the App you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information. Your name and email address when you create an account (via email/password, Sign in with Google, or Sign in with Apple). With Sign in with Apple you may choose to hide your email via Apple’s private relay.
  • Phone number. Collected to verify your account via a one-time password (OTP).
  • Identity verification data. To prevent fraud and confirm you are a genuine, present person, we collect government-ID images (e.g. national ID), a short live selfie, and information read from your ID (such as your legal name). See “Biometric and Face Data” below.
  • Listing and content data. Property details, photos, unit layouts, and documents you upload when listing or inquiring about a property.
  • Communications. Messages and voice calls you exchange with other users through the App. Voice calls may be recorded to support safety and our anti-broker policy; you are notified in-app that a call may be recorded.
  • Location. Approximate/area location you provide or select to search and filter property listings. We do not collect or track your device’s GPS location, and we do not use location for advertising.
  • Device and technical data. A push-notification token and basic technical information needed to operate the App (e.g. to deliver messages and call notifications).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and secure your account and authenticate you.
  • To verify your identity and phone number, and to prevent fraud and abuse.
  • To publish your listings and enable messaging, connection requests, and calls between users.
  • To send you notifications related to your activity (e.g. new messages, connection requests, calls).
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the App.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your information for third-party advertising, we do not sell your information, and we do not track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

3. Biometric and Face Data

To verify your identity and prevent fraud, Mullak performs an identity check during account verification. With your participation, we capture a short live selfie and a photo of your face, and compare them with the photo on your government-issued ID to confirm you are a real, present person and that you match your ID. This processing is performed on our behalf by Amazon Web Services (AWS Rekognition) as our data processor, in the AWS Europe (Ireland) region; your verification images are stored in our private, access-controlled storage in the AWS Europe (Milan) region.

What we keep and for how long. Your face and ID images are retained only for as long as needed to complete and maintain verification, and are deleted when you delete your account. To prevent a user who has been rejected or banned from creating a new account to evade our broker/fraud controls, we also generate and retain an irreversible numeric representation of your face (a mathematical template that cannot be reversed to reconstruct your face or produce an image of you). This representation is used solely for fraud and duplicate-account prevention and for no other purpose — it is never used for advertising, profiling, tracking, or personalization, and is never sold or shared with data brokers.

4. How We Share Information

  • With other users. Your listings and the profile/contact information necessary to transact (e.g. when you message or call another user) are shared with that user.
  • With service providers (processors). We use trusted providers that process data on our behalf and under contract, including Amazon Web Services (identity verification, storage, and infrastructure), our authentication provider (SuperTokens), our messaging/calls infrastructure, and push-notification services (Apple Push Notification service and Firebase Cloud Messaging). They may process data only to provide their service to us.
  • For legal reasons. Where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share it with data brokers.

5. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. When you delete your account, we delete your personal data and your verification images, retaining only the irreversible, anonymized face representation described above for fraud prevention, and any records we are required to keep by law.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • Account deletion. You can permanently delete your account at any time from within the App at Settings → Account → Delete account. Deletion is permanent and removes your login and personal data.
  • Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us using the details below to exercise them.

7. Security

We use access controls and encryption in transit to protect your data, and store sensitive verification data in private, access-controlled storage. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children and is intended for users who are of legal age to enter into property transactions. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in the European Union (AWS Europe — Ireland and Milan regions) and other locations where our service providers operate, with appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App.
